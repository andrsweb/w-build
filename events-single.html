AN EVENING OF SEASON & STORY

Inspired by ancient midsummer celebrations and the summer solstice, A Summer’s Evening: A Feast with Faeries and Firelight transforms Waverly Springs across two enchanting evenings in July.

Structured in four acts and twelve courses, the experience unfolds gradually through music, movement, atmosphere, and moments of surprise woven throughout the estate. Designed around the lingering light of the solstice, the evening invites guests to settle in, wander slowly, and remain awhile.

AN EVENING IN FOUR ACTS

A dinner staged like a play. Each act unfolds in a new corner of the property; each course, a scene in a story written by the season itself. Over the evening the experience moves gradually through shifting settings and changing light.

Act I

Faery Hour & Strolling Reception

The evening opens outdoors in the lingering light of the solstice. Seasonal pours, early courses, and music drift through the gardens as guests settle into the rhythm of the night.

Act II

THRESHOLD & TABLE ADORNMENT

As daylight fades, the experience moves indoors. Long tables, flickering candlelight, layered courses, and a transformed dining room bring warmth and intimacy to the heart of the evening.

Act III

FIRE & SMOKE

The third act deepens into something moodier and more atmospheric. Firelight, smoke, music, and unexpected moments emerge across the estate as the night stretches on.

Act IV

BONFIRE & CORDIALS

Dessert arrives slowly beneath the summer sky as music, movement, and firelight carry the evening toward its final moments, inviting guests to remain awhile beneath the solstice night.
